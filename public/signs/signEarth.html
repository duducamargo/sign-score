Signs of Earth

Earth

If we could summarize the Earth element in one word, perhaps the most appropriate one would be practicality.

"Earth defines the action of the stars with practicality and pragmatism. Planets act according to materiality and reality, recognizing their own limits and gradually advancing toward their goals. The tone is the pursuit of security and stability," says astrologer Leonardo Lemos.

On the other hand, according to Leonardo, when a chart does not show the Earth element, "there is a difficulty in dealing with limits, concretizing and giving shape to their efforts." In this case, the person stays too much in the world of ideas and too little in the world of applications.

However, although there are common characteristics among people of the Earth element in general, it manifests with some peculiarities depending on the sign. This is because reading the Natal Chart cannot be done by looking at just one piece of information. You have to see the whole picture.

Taurus

The sign of Taurus symbolizes the steadfastness of Earth. Taureans tend to be calm and persistent, with a great appreciation for their possessions and material goods.

On the other hand, excessive "steadfastness" can result in stubbornness. Or, even difficulty accepting change.

Venus is the ruling planet of Taurus, relating the sign to loving in a caring and affectionate way. Being in the company of Taureans reveals someone who loves in a practical and gentle manner.

Virgo

Virgos are loyal to the people they love, whether they are family, friends, or a partner. Sincere and mature, they usually have no trouble expressing their feelings.

Virgo is ruled by Mercury, which highlights its critical and opinionated nature.

Capricorn

Capricorn individuals possess strong Earth element characteristics, with well-defined goals and an understanding of their limits. For them, success comes through persistence, learning, and discipline.

However, if they don't know how to control their excessive material focus, they may develop attitudes like greed and pessimism.

With Saturn as its ruling planet, the sign of Capricorn naturally understands that everything has its time.
